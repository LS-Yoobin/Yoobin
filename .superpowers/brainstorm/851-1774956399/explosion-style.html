Panel Shards

The text and UI elements fracture into rectangular fragments — like a HUD screen taking a hit. Geometric, military, precise. Shards drift upward and fade. Feels like tech breaking apart.

Energy Sparks

The hero dissolves into glowing point particles — like a ship's systems shorting out. Dozens of tiny neon sparks burst upward from where text was. Feels electric and energetic.

Shards + Sparks (Recommended)

A few large panel fragments fly out while a burst of tiny sparks scatter upward — the way a real sci-fi explosion looks. The crosshair rings also break apart. Most cinematic of the three.

All three styles are scroll-driven and progressive — the faster you scroll, the more dramatic the effect.
